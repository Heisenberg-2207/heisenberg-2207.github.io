Parallel 2D DFT
2D Discrete Fourier Transform implemented from scratch in C/C++ — no FFTW, no NumPy. Parallelized with OpenMP (shared memory) and MPI (distributed). Achieved over 80% efficiency with near-linear speedup.

EEG Graph Classification of Schizophrenia
GCNN pipeline for schizophrenia detection from resting-state EEG. Correlation graphs built from PSD features across delta, theta, and alpha bands. Evaluated under eyes-open and eyes-closed conditions using leave-one-out cross-validation. Collaboration with Mount Sinai Hospital.
