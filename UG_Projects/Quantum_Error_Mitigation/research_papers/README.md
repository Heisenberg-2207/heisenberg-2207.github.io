Reference papers consulted during this project, covering ML-based quantum error mitigation and related reinforcement-learning approaches to quantum control/error correction.

| `RL-QEC_Harnessing_Reinforcement_Learning_for_Quantum_Error_Correction_Advancements.pdf` | RL-QEC: Harnessing Reinforcement Learning for Quantum Error Correction Advancements | Veeresh K, Deepak S, Srinivas T (Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore) |

These directly motivate the project's central question — whether a trained ML model can replace multi-execution Zero-Noise Extrapolation with a single-shot, learned correction.
